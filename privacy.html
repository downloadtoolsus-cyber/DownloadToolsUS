Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

This page explains how the Chrome extension Aliexpress image downloader handles your data.

What the extension does

The extension helps you download images and videos from AliExpress product pages for your own use (for example, for product pages or ad creatives).

Data we collect

The extension is designed to work locally in your browser.

By default:

  • We do not collect or store your browsing history.
  • We do not collect or store the files you download.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

If you add any optional features in the future that send data to a server (for example, analytics or cloud storage), describe them clearly here.

Permissions used by the extension

The extension requests permissions only to perform its core function:

  • Access to AliExpress pages – needed to read image and video URLs on AliExpress product pages so they can be downloaded.
  • Download permission – needed to save images and videos to your device using the Chrome downloads API.

The extension does not access pages or data on other websites unless you grant such access in your browser settings.

How downloads work

  • Media files are downloaded directly from AliExpress to your device.
  • The extension does not upload your files to any external server.
  • You can delete downloaded files at any time from your computer.

Third-party services

The extension may be distributed through the Chrome Web Store and is subject to Google’s own terms and policies.

AliExpress and Alibaba Group are separate third parties. This extension is not endorsed by or affiliated with AliExpress or Alibaba Group.

Children’s privacy

The extension is built for adult users such as store owners and marketers. It is not aimed at children and should not be used by children.

Changes to this policy

This policy may change in the future. When it changes, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be updated.
